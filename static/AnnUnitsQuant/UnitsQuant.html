Units and Quantities


What is the meaning of unit and quantity?
Well, quantity, also called dimension, is what you measure, i.e. any measurable extent or magnitude such as length, mass, time, etc. A unit is a "yardstick" by which you express your measurements. For example, a measurement of the length quantity can be expressed as a number of meter units.
